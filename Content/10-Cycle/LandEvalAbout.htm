The Planning Cycle - Landscape Evaluation

The Landscape Evaluation stage of the Planning Cycle walks you through the different tasks in that are required to evaluate your landscape. The tasks in this stage of the cycle help you better understand your landscape, its features, and develop goals to reduce risk. These tasks include:

  • Create a Landscape
  • Edit or Correct a Landscape
  • Create an Automatic 97th Percentile Landscape Fire Behavior (Auto97th LFB) Report
  • Map Values
  • Calculate Landscape Fire Behavior

More capability on assessing landscape burn probabilities and developing project objectives will be available in future version of !
