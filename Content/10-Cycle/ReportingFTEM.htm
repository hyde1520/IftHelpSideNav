Fuels Treatment Effectiveness Modeling (FTEM) Reporting in the  Planning Cycle

From the Reporting stage of the Planning Cycle, federal land managers can produce reports on the interaction of wildfires with fuels treatments for FTEM.

FTEM reports compile and summarize monitoring data for completed Wildfires for specific dates, regions and agencies. Report types include:

  • FTEM Wildfire Report
  • FTEM Agency Summary
  • FTEM Region Summary
  • FTEM State Summary
  • Completed Wildfire Monitoring Data

See the Help Center topic on FTEM Reporting for instructions on FTEM Reporting, or the FTEM "About" topic to learn more general information about FTEM.
