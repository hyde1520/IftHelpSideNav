Fuels Treatment Effectiveness Monitoring (FTEM) in the  Planning Cycle

From the Monitoring stage of the Planning Cycle, federal land managers can monitor the interaction of wildfires with fuels treatments for FTEM. The FTEM monitoring walks through the process of selecting a wildfire, select treatments, entering monitoring data, and reviewing and completing your monitoring.

See the Help Center topic on Using FTEM for instructions on FTEM Monitoring, or the FTEM "About" topic to learn more general information about FTEM.
