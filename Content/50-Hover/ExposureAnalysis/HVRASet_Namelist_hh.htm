This lists the Primary HVRA categories and Sub-HVRAs that comprise the HVRA Set you selected for Exposure Analysis.

If your HVRA Set is larger than the extent of your selected Landscape Burn Probability (LBP) model output, you will be prompted to create a new version of the HVRA Set that is clipped to the extent of your selected LBP model output. This will be done without leaving the Exposure Analysis page.

Note: Sub-HVRAs in red italics indicate that the particular Sub-HVRA is outside the extent of the LBP, and will not be included in the new clipped HVRA Set.
