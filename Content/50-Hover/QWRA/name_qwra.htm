Give your output a descriptive name. You may choose to:

  • Save: Save your inputs and continue the QWRA at a later time.
  • Run: Run the QWRA with the inputs you enter
  • Update: The Update button replaces the Save button if you begin to edit a saved QWRA. Update will save your new changes and overwrite your previous inputs.

A QWRA is stored in the same folder in My workspace as the Exposure Analysis output used in that QWRA.
