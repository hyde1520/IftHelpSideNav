Select a shape or shapefile (it may be multipart if needed) to indicate the location and pattern of ignition on the landscape. You may use points, lines, or polygons for this task.

You may upload or create an ignition file in this interface if you do not yet have one, the drop-down menus will automatically update.

To ensure successful MTT run completion:

  • Do not use ignition files that land completely in unburnable fuels or within a barrier.

  • If barriers are also used, ensure barriers and ignitions are at least one cell width away from each other.

Important considerations include:

  • If a polygon is used, MTT treats the area within the polygon as non-burnable; fire is not permitted to burn inward.

  • Once MTT is run, the ignition file will be converted to a raster format with the same resolution as the rest of the landscape.

  • Once a shape or shapefile is selected for the ignition input, it may not be used for the barrier input.

See the Ignition topic for more information.
