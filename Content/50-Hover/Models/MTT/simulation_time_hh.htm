Simulation Time specifies the duration of the fire growth for the set of fuel moisture and wind conditions specified. Simulation Time is input in two steps, the first specifying the burn period length, and the second specifying the number of of burn periods.

Burn Period Length: This describes the duration of fire activity for each burn period, in hours (1-24).

Number of Simulations: This describes the number for burn periods (1-5) for your simulation.

See the Simulation Time topic for more information.
