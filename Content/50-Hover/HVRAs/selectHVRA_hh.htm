Primary HVRAs are used to label and organize your sub-HVRAs. Sub-HVRAs are the actual shapes.

Choose your Primary HVRAs using the Primary HVRA Category drop-down menu, or create your own with the Add New HVRA button.

With a Primary HVRA selected, select the sub-HVRAs that comprise it. Initially you will see a list of all available sub-HVRAs, but you may use the Reference Data button or User Data buttons to filter the options.

  • Indicates the sub-HVRA is derived from National HVRA data.
