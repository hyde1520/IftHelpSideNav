Version 3.2.1 Exposure Analysis – Released March 6 2020

Version 3.2.1 includes Exposure Analysis which is used to quickly summarize Landscape Burn Probability (LBP)outputs across the extent of a Highly Valued Resources or Asset Set (HVRA Set):

  • Run Exposure Analysis to summarize burn probability, conditional flame length, and integrated hazard across the extent of your Highly Valued Resources or Asset Sets (HVRA Sets).
  • Need to re-run an LBP model on a treated landscape to compare results? Now you can select an ignition profile from a previous run to replicate the exact LBP model conditions on a treated landscape.
  • Phased Release - Exposure Analysis is one in a series of phased releases that will facilitate Quantitative Wildfire Risk Assessment.

Coming in 2020

Quantitative Wildfire Risk Assessment - Under Development

A workflow and tools to perform a Quantitative Wildfire Risk Assessment (QWRA) from the project to the unit scale is under development in IFTDSS. The process follows the steps outlined in GTR-315, “A wildfire risk assessment framework for land and resource management.” IFTDSS is unique in that it will contain a comparison functionality allowing users to complete a QWRA and then compare treatment alternatives to understand the potential impacts on risk.
