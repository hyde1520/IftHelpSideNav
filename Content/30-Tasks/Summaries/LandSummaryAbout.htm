Generate an Automatic 97th Percentile Landscape Fire Behavior (Auto97th LFB) Report

Always review the Auto97th-generated fire weather parameters and fire behavior outputs to ensure they are representative of problem fire/worst-case conditions for your area.

Automatic 97th Percentile Landscape Fire Behavior (Auto97th LFB) Report outline landscape features and predicted worst-case fire behavior (97th percentile fuel moisture and wind). These high level landscape summaries are intended as a starting point for evaluating your area as part of the fuels management planning process.

Below, we discuss creating summary reports from the Landscape Planning Cycle in . There is also a button that appears under selected landscapes in My Workspace labeled "Request a Report". That button only generates a summary of landscape features, not the additional Auto97th LFB Report that we discuss here from the Landscape Planning Cycle.

Navigate to Landscape Summary
  1. Go to the Planning Cycle from the top navigation menu.
  2. Click the Landscape Evaluation Stage of the Cycle.

  3. Click the Landscape Summary button.

 Creating an Auto97th LFB Report Saving & Printing a Landscape Summary

Saving

In addition to being saved in My Workspace, you may also save summary reports to your local computer. Using the download button in the top right will download the entire report as a .pdf file. Clicking the options button to the right of each section of the report, will save that specific image or graph.

Printing from your internet browser

Summary reports can also be printed directly from the internet browser. To print the full summary using Google Chrome:

  1. Right click anywhere within the white space of summary, in this example, we've clicked to the right of the summary title. This brings up several options, including Print

  2. Click Print from the options and follow your normal routine for printing a document from your computer.

 Using Auto97th Outputs in Other Models to Determine Worst-Case Fuel Moistures or Create Other Runs

Auto97th reports are useful for quickly gathering 97th Percentile fuel moistures to use in other analyses or as a baseline for additional model runs where you may want to keep most of the auto97th values but change some.

Quickly find Auto97 Fuel Moistures

  1. Navigate to Modeling Playground.
  2. Locate your Auto97 run and click on it to view file options.
  3. Click the View Input button.

  4. A pop-up appears with all model inputs including Initial Fuel Moistures. To close the information box, click OK in the bottom right.

Copy an auto97 model run and re-run with changes

  1. Navigate to Modeling Playground.
  2. Locate your Auto97 run and click on it to view file options.
  3. Click the Copy button.

  4. A new model input screen appears pre-populated with all the inputs used for the Auto97th run. You may edit any of these inputs, give the run a new name, and save and run the model.
