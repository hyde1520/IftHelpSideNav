About Quantitative Risk Assessment

Quantitative Risk Assessment describes the process of analyzing the risks posed by wildfire to highly valued resource areas.

 

Throughout 2019 Quantitative Risk Assessment will be added to upcoming IFTDSS versions:

  • Version 3.2.0: Landscape Burn Probability Model added to Modeling Playground. Model landscape burn probability, conditional flame lengths, and integrated hazard, view the results in Map Studio, as a summary report, or download them to your local computer for use in other applications.
  • Version 3.2.1 Single Landscape Integrated Hazard Assessment: Map Highly Valued Resource Areas (HVRAs) and generate an integrated hazard report.
  • Version 3.2.2 Quantitative Wildfire Risk Assessment: Define response functions and relative importance for HVRAs, map risk outputs, generate a risk summary report, and download risk outputs
  • Version 3.2.3 Compare Integrated Hazard or Risk: Complete an integrated hazard or risk assessment for related landscapes, generate comparison grids and comparison reports
