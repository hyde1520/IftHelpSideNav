Map HVRAs

From the Strategic Planning stage of the Planning Cycle:

Select the Map HVRAs task.

Select the geographic extent by choosing a landscape from the drop-down menu.

(Optional) further constrain your geographic extent by choosing an Area of Interest (shape or shapefile).

Select a Primary HVRA Category from the drop-down menu.

Select a Sub-HVRA from the drop-down menu.

To add the HVRA, click Add to Pending Mapped Values.

Give the Mapped Values a name and click Save.
