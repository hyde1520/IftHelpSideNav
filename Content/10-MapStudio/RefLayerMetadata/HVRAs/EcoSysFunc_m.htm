Overview

Ecosystem Function is part of the Highly Valued Resources or Assets (HVRA) data used in the National Wildfire Risk Assessment for Forest Service Lands (NaWRA, Dillon In Press). Ecosystem Function can be used as sub-HVRAs within the Map Values, Exposure Analysis and Quantitative Wildfire Risk Assessment functionality within the Interagency Fuel Treatment Decision Support System (IFTDSS, https://iftdss.firenet.gov).

Data Details

Broad ecology and role of fire in ecosystems. Derived from LANDFIRE Biophysical Settings (BpS, version 1.2.0) data. Consolidated the XXXX BpS’ into 84 groupings based on ecological setting, historic fire frequency, and general fire ecology. Then further lumped the data into 12 groups based on the response functions, or the effect of fire on each group.
