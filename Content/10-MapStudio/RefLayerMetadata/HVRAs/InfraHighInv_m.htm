Overview

Infrastructure - High Investment Buildings and Developed Recreation Sites is part of the Highly Valued Resources or Assets (HVRA) data used in the National Wildfire Risk Assessment for Forest Service Lands (NaWRA, Dillon In Press). High Investment Buildings and Developed Recreation Sites can be used as a sub-HVRA within the Map Values, Exposure Analysis and Quantitative Wildfire Risk Assessment functionality within the Interagency Fuel Treatment Decision Support System (, https://iftdss.firenet.gov).

Data Processing

High Investment Buildings and Developed Recreation Sites was created using tow datasets that are part of the USDA Forest Service Enterprise Data. The Building Feature Class provides descriptive and location information about existing Forest Service owned buildings and existing non-FS owned buildings that are located on Forest Service lands. The Recreation Sites Feature Class provides descriptive and location information about existing Forest Service recreation sites such as Campgrounds, Picnic Areas, Trailheads.
