FTEM Reference Layers

Use the Add Layers widget to add reference layers to the FTEM map. In addition to the Reference layers available in the rest of IFTDSS, there are specific layers only available in FTEM:

FTEM Treatment Points

  • All Treatment Points
  • BIA FTEM Treatment Points
  • BLM FTEM Treatment Points
  • NPS FTEM Treatment Points
  • USFS FTEM Treatment Points
  • USFWS FTEM Treatment Points

FTEM Treatment Points

  • All Treatment Polys
  • BIA FTEM Treatment Polys
  • BLM FTEM Treatment Polys
  • NPS FTEM Treatment Polys
  • USFS FTEM Treatment Polys
  • USFWS FTEM Treatment Polys
