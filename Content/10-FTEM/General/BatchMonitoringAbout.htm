About Batch Monitoring

Batch monitoring allows you to populate a spreadsheet with monitoring data for one or more wildfires and upload that data to FTEM.

There are two methods of batch monitoring depending on how you choose to narrow your wildfire list.

  • From the monitoring interface: Change the map extent until the Wildfire list reflects the fires you need to monitor, then generate a fillable spreadsheet to monitor those fires.

  • From the reporting interface: Use a series of report filters to generate a fillable spreadsheet to monitor those fires.
