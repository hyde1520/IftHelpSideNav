Finding Fire Information in FTEM

Wildfire information, including start date can be viewed either by using the Identify widget, or the right hand panel.

Viewing Fire Information with Identify

  1. Locate your fire.
  2. Click the Identify widget.

  3. Click the fire to display the information.

Viewing Fire Information in the Right Hand Panel

  1. Use the split screen button to view the fire list in full screen model.

  2. Clicking on a fire will show fire metadata in the right hand panel.
