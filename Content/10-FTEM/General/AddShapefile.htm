Adding Shapefiles in FTEM

For wildfires with no perimeter (polygon) showing in FTEM, you may find it helpful to upload and view a polygon using a local file and the Upload Shapefile widget on the FTEM map.

Note that this will not save your shapefile, it is intended to be viewed while working with wildfires in treatments in the FTEM system. To permanently add wildfires and treatments to FTEM, please contact your FTEM Agency Administrator, or visit the FAQ section addressing why fire perimeters may not be displayed.

To add a shapefile from your local computer:

  1. Click the Upload Shapefile widget.

  2. Click Upload File.

    The FTEM Upload Shapefile feature adds a shapefile to the map interface from your local computer. To add your shapefile, it must be compressed as a .zip file, and contain at least shp, .shx, .dbf, and .prj files.

  3. Search your local computer file directory, select it, and click Open.

  4. Check any fields to include.

  5. Click Add to Map.

  6. Your shapefile is added to the map and you are automatically zoomed to its extent.
