Add Treatments Using the Map

Treatments can be added to a wildfire both by searching a buffer around the fire and selecting treatments, or by adding the treatment from the map interface. These instructions describe the latter.

  1. With your fire selected, open the map Attribute Table.
  2. Treatment layers are listed along the top of the table, click a layer to populate the table with data from that layer.
  3. To better see a treatment you are viewing in the attribute table, highlight it by clicking the gray area to the left of the treatment row.

  4. Click Zoom To.

  5. From the Layer List, click More Options to the right of the treatment layer name, and ensure "Enable Pop-up" is selected from the drop-down menu.

  6. Scroll over the treatment and left click it.

  7. In the description that appears, use the Add Interaction option to add that treatment to the list of interacting treatments for that fire.
