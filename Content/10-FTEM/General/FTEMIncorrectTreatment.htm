Incorrect Treatments

Totally unlinked to anything, if we want to keep this page I'll tie it into to the appropriate place. - This may be useful as a stand alone link for Help Tix and FAQs

Incorrect treatments in FTEM are usually caused by error in the data sources FTEM pulls from.

Treatments cannot be corrected in FTEM but must be corrected in the systems FTEM pulls from, either IRWIN or NIFS.

To correct a treatment, contact your GIS Specialist to update the information in FACTS or NFPORS. The correction will then be pulled into FTEM with 24 hours of the update.
