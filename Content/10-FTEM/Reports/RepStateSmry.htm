FTEM State Summary

This summary is generated for a specific state and interaction year and describes the fire and treatment interactions in each land management agency for that year and state.

Report Contents

Lists for each agency:

  • Recorded treatments that did and did not change fire behavior.
  • Recorded treatments that did and did not help control wildfire.
  • Recorded treatments that did and did not change or control fire behavior.
  • Total number of records.

Report Download Options

  • Excel Spreadsheet (xlsx)

Right click the Example State Summary Report link and select "Save link as" in your browser to save an example to your computer in xlsx format.
