Generating and Viewing Reports

In My Workspace, you may generate a summary report to assess landscape features and model outputs. Reports contain a variety of graphs and tables that can each be downloaded individually to use in your own documents. You may also choose to download the entire report as a PDF.

Generate a Report

View and Save Reports

  1. Reports can be viewed anytime from My Workspace by selecting the report and clicking the View Summary button in the list of file options that appears when you click the report file.

  2. Reports open in a new browser tab. You may choose to scroll through the report, or use the linked table of contents on the left side of the report to skip to a specific section. Download the entire report as a PDF by clicking the download button in the top right, or download an individual graph or image by clicking the download button in the top right of the graph or image.

    • Report maps download as a JPG file.
    • Report graphs can download as JPG, PNG, or PDF file. You will be prompted to choose one after clicking the download button.

  3. Report tables can be adjusted to show a specific number of rows per page, copied to be pasted into other documents, printed, or saved as a CSV, Excel, or PDF file.
