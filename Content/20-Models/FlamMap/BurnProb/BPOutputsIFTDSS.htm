Landscape Burn Probability outputs are available as geospatial layers in Map Studio (added to the map using the Simulation Output Tools widget).

Band # Output Units Brief Description Map Studio Report
1 1-hr Fuel Moisture Percent Moisture content of 1-hour fuel (dead woody fuel 0 to ¼ inch or 0 to 6.4 mm in diameter) is the mass of water within the fuel particle expressed as a percentage of the fuel particle’s oven-dry mass. x  
2 10-hr Fuel Moisture Percent Moisture content of 10-hour fuel (dead woody fuel ¼ to 1 inch or 0.6 to 2.5 cm in diameter) is the mass of water within the fuel particle expressed as a percentage of the fuel particle’s oven-dry mass. x  
3 100-hr Fuel Moisture Percent Moisture content of 100-hour fuel (dead woody fuel 1 to 3 inches or 2.5 to 7.6 cm in diameter) is the mass of water within the fuel particle expressed as a percentage of the fuel particle’s oven-dry mass. x  
4 Mid-Flame Wind Speed Miles per Hour Wind velocity at the mid-flame height. Calculated using wind adjustment factors based on stand height and canopy cover and the Wind Speed. x  
5 Wind Speed Miles per Hour Wind velocity measured 20 feet (6 m) above the top of the tree or shrub canopy, or above the ground if canopy is absent.    
6 Wind Direction Degrees Wind direction is the direction from which the wind is blowing, specified in degrees increasing clockwise from north (which is zero degrees).    
7 Burn Probability Unitless The likelihood that a fire will burn a given point on the landscape. See the Burn Probability topic for more details. x x
8 Conditional Flame Length Feet The mean flame length of all the fires that burn a given point on the landscape. See the Conditional Flame Length topic for more details. x x
9 Proportion of Flame Lengths 0-2 ft Unitless The probability of fire burning with a flame length between >0 and 2 ft. See the Flame Length Probability topic for more details.    
11 Proportion of Flame Lengths 2-4 ft Unitless The probability of fire burning with a flame length between >4 and 6 ft. See the Flame Length Probability topic for more details.    
12 Proportion of Flame Lengths 4-6 ft Unitless The probability of fire burning with a flame length between >4 and 6 ft. See the Flame Length Probability topic for more details.    
13 Proportion of Flame Lengths 6-8 ft Unitless The probability of fire burning with a flame length between >6 and 8 ft. See the Flame Length Probability topic for more details.    
14 Proportion of Flame Lengths 8-12 ft Unitless The probability of fire burning with a flame length between >8 and 12 ft. See the Flame Length Probability topic for more details.    
10 Proportion of Flame Lengths >12 ft Unitless The probability of fire burning with a flame length between >12 ft. See the Flame Length Probability topic for more details.
