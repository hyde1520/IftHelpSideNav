LFB GeoTIFF download differences for non-burnable pixels

Due to updates to the FlamMap basic fire behavior model within , the values for non-burnable pixels are listed differently in the GeoTIFF downloaded, depending upon the date the download was generated. Currently, fuel moisture and crown fraction burned are "0.000000" for all non-burnable pixels regardless of whether fuel moisture conditioning was used. For values prior to 6/24/19, please refer to the table below.
