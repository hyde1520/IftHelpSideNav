Landscapes for LBP

When choosing a landscape for LBP, only those landscapes with a buffer and unaltered resolution are available from the drop-down menu. (Landscapes with the suffix “NB” or “Xm” will not run with LBP).

Avoid comparing outputs across analyses. Use a landscape that includes the entire area for which you may want to compare results. In most cases this would be a project area boundary but in some cases could be larger to encompass an entire ownership area (district or park).
