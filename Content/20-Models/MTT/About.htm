About Minimum Travel Time (MTT)

Minimum travel time (MTT) simulates fire spread and behavior over the landscape. Use multiple points and lines to represent both ignition points and ignition barriers. Running MTT on your treated and untreated landscape can provide useful estimates of how treatments may influence fire spread.
