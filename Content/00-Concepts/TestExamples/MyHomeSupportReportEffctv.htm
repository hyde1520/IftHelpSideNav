Monitoring Treatment Effectiveness

Federal fire managers may be tasked with reporting the effectiveness of their fuel treatments if a wildfire burns through the treatment area. This is performed through the Fuels Treatment Effectiveness Monitoring (FTEM) interface:

  1. Use the Wildfire tab to locate the wildfire which burned through the area.
  2. Review the FTEM treatment list on the Treatment tab, adding or removing to it as necessary.
  3. On the Monitoring tab, monitor treatment effectiveness by answering the questions and attaching any necessary files.
  4. Complete the monitoring from the Complete tab.
  5. Direct any supervisors who require data to the Reporting section of the Fuels Treatment Effectiveness Monitoring (FTEM) interface.
