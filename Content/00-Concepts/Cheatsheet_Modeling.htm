Getting Started: Model Runs

Introduction

Any model available in may be run from Modeling Playground. Use the drop-down menu at the top to select a model, then proceed by clicking the Create Run.

Currently the models available in focus on Fire behavior and include:

  • Landscape Fire Behavior
  • Landscape Burn Probability for Quantitative Wildfire Risk Assessment
  • Minimum Travel Time
  • Minimum Travel Time
  • More to come....
