Getting Started: Navigating

The main components of the application are outlined below. These can be accessed anytime from the top navigation bar. They include:

My Home

This is the page you first arrive on after logging into . It contains a short introduction paragraph to as well as shortcut buttons to other parts of .

 My Workspace

My Workspace: This is a list of all your files used in . These can be organized into folders. Clicking on the name of any file brings up options such as edit, delete, and view on map. My Workspace is also available from the Continue Working button on My Home. The files available in My Workspace include:

  • Landscape files (.lcp files) which is the basic file used throughout to represent your landscape.
  • Shapefiles that you upload and those that you create within .

 The Fuels Planning Cycle

The Fuels Planning Cycle: The planning cycle walks you through the steps of landscape planning, such as landscape evaluation, strategic planning, implementation, and reporting. Each stage of the planning cycle walks you through the steps necessary to complete it. Each step takes you to the part of the application needed to complete it. The Planning Cycle can also be accessed from the Start New button on My Home.

 My Modeling Playground

My Modeling Playground: Here, you choose which models you'd like to run, and then begin modeling. All model outputs are listed in My Modeling Playground. Clicking on a model output name brings up options such as Copy, View Input, Download, Delete, or View on Map (see results displayed in Map Studio). My Modeling Playground may also be accessed via the Modeling Playground button on My Home.

 Map Studio

Map Studio: This is the mapping interface of , where you can create and edit landscape and shape files, view modeling outputs spatially, and compare various landscape features and modeling outputs across your landscape. This is available on navigation bar at the top of .

 Help

You may access the Help Center anytime by clicking the Help icon in the top right of the menu bar.

Help Center: will take you to the Help Center main page and search bar. From here you can use the links, or search bar, to find full-length help pages, videos, tutorials, and technical documentation.

About this Page:will take you to the specific topic in Help Center that addresses the page you're using in , generally a step by step walk-though with video links.

As you use Help Center will notice Warnings, Notes, Tips, Announcements:

Warnings indicate information you need to know to successfully complete a task or avoid interpretation errors.

Notes include helpful information that applies to the task you are performing.

Tips include information that will save time, or make subsequent processes in easier.

Announcements describe features that will be added to in subsequent versions.

Q & A Forums - takes you to the Users forum where you can get help from other users.

Help Tour - Displays short tips and links within the interface.

There are also help embedded in the interface, either small question mark icons that display very brief tips and links when clicked, or help information displayed in the right-hand panel.
